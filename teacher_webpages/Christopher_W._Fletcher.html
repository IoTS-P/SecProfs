In Brief

Hi there! My name is Chris Fletcher (a.k.a. 'C Fletch', or just 'Fletch', or just 'C F'). I am an Associate Professor at the University of California, Berkeley. Before that I spent six wonderful years at UIUC as an Assistant Professor, took a year as a research post-doc at NVIDIA, got a Ph.D. at MIT and, finally, a B.S. also at Berkeley.

I am always looking for talented PhD students. If you are interested in joining my group, please make sure to apply to Berkeley in December, and list me under 'Faculty Interest'. Please do not email directly me about PhD supervision before you have been admitted (unless we have met in some professional context).
