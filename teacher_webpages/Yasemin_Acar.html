about

I'm not the only Yasemin Acar. I am not the successful UK-based professor, and I am not the Berlin-based activist who's recently been on the news. I would like to remind you that sending threats and insults via email is a crime, so kindly consider not sending them. I do appreciate emails about research.

I am a professor in computer science at Paderborn University in Germany, and a research assistant professor at The George Washington University in Washington DC. I previously co-lead the GWUSec lab, and prior to that, the Human Factors in Security and Privacy Group at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy in Bochum, Germany.


If you are interested in my work or wish to collaborate with me, please do not hesitate to contact me.
