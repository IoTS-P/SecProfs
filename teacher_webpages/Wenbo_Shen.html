    Short Bio

    Dr. Wenbo Shen is a ZJU100 Young Professor at Zhejiang University. His research interests are operating system security, container security, and software supply chain security. He has published over 50 research papers at top-tier academic conferences. Dr. Wenbo Shen is a recipient of three distinguished paper awards (NDSS 16, AsiaCCS 17, ACSAC 22). His research work of Real-time Kernel Protection (RKP) has been deployed on hundreds of millions of devices.

    Research
