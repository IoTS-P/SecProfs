Daniel Gruss

 

I'm a university professor in the Secure Systems group (Team Gruss/CoreSec) at the Graz University of Technology, Institute of Applied Information Processing and Communications. In my research I explore software-based microarchitectural attacks and operating system features. I teach undergraduate courses such as Operating Systems (see Hall of Fame), System-Level Programming, Information Security, and Introduction to Scientific Working, and, together with my PhD students, graduate courses such as Side Channel Security, Secure Software Development, Cloud Operating Systems.
