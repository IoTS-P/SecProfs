I founded and lead the CUHK Computer Security Lab.

I am generally interested in all problems related to computer security and privacy, including the ones intersecting with other areas in computer science and other disciplines. Most recently, I have been focusing on protecting the applications/services and the end users on the Internet.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2017, and my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2012.
