Biography

I am an associate professor of College of Computing and Data Science at Nanyang Technological University. Before that I spent two years at Amazon.com working on cloud computing, IoT and smarthome systems. I received my Ph.D in Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University in 2017, and Bachelor's degree at Peking University. I have a broad interest in building trustworthy, efficient and intelligent systems. For more details, please check my research projects and publications.
