The CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security is a German national Big Science institution within the Helmholtz Association. CISPA researchers explore all aspects of information security. They address the pressing global challenges in cybersecurity, data protection and trustworthy artificial intelligence. Conducting modern foundational research as well as innovative application-oriented research, they work to protect the digital space and improve industrial applications and products. CISPA promotes scientific talent, supports promising founders, and trains experts and executives for business and industry.
